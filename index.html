Ayodhya is an ancient city in India that is considered one of the seven sacred cities of the Hindus. It is located on the Ghaghara River, east of Faizabad. Ayodhya is also known as Saket.

Ayodhya is the birthplace of Rama and the setting of the epic poem Ramayana. The city was the capital of the ancient Kosala Kingdom. According to the Ramayana, Ayodhya was founded by Manu, the progenitor of mankind.

The Ayodhya Ram Mandir is expected to be completed on January 24, 2024. The temple is being built by the Larsen & Toubro group and is estimated to cost Rs 18,000 crore.

Ayodhya Upcoming Events

Ram Navami is a significant Hindu festival celebrated with great enthusiasm and devotion to honor the birth of Lord Rama, the seventh incarnation of Lord Vishnu. It falls on the ninth day (Navami) of the Chaitra month in the Hindu lunar calendar, typically in March or April of the Gregorian calendar. In 2024, Ram Navami will be observed on Wed, 17 Apr, 2024.

A member of the temple construction committee has said that the opening of the Ram temple in Ayodhya is planned to happen between January 14 and January 24. The ceremonies to get ready for the opening will begin on January 14, and the exact opening date will be decided after getting confirmation from Prime Minister Narendra Modi.

Ayodhya, nestled on the banks of the sacred Sarayu River, is hailed as the birthplace of Lord Ram, the seventh incarnation of Lord Vishnu. This ancient city resonates with historical and cultural importance, casting a spellbinding aura on visitors and believers alike. With its rich mythology and numerous sacred sites, Ayodhya is an embodiment of divine grace.
